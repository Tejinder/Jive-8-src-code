Objectives

Deep Blue+ is a pricing research tool to forecast brand shares and movements due to any price change. However, the possible usage of Deep Blue+ is much beyond the area of pricing research. It is a suite of market research tool to help BAT end markets to take strategic pricing decisions through portfolio profit optimisation and provides a platform to explore

1. Deep Blue+ enables the BAT End Market to take informed pricing decisions for its current portfolio of offers in isolation or in the competitive context.

The key purposes of Deep Blue+ as a price forecasting tool are to:

  • Determine price elasticity of different brands
  • Predict brand share movements for current offers, cannibalisation and share of draw
  • Evaluate different possible pricing scenarios and their impact on brand share

2. Deep Blue+ enables the business to identify pricing scenarios that could optimise BAT’s portfolio position through volume, revenue or profit maximization, either individually or in any combination, taking into account competitor response.

This may be achieved through analysing consumers’ value assessment of the current offerings in the market and by checking if there is any scope for altering the current portfolio pricing to optimise portfolio position under given constraints.

3. Deep Blue+ enhances the framework of consumer value based pricing through evaluation of differentiated feature based innovations among target consumers. It principally tries to achieve the following:

  • Estimate consumer delight, i.e. the premium consumers are willing to pay over and above the current price of a brand for differentiated features
  • Decide on optimum price for these new feature based innovative offers
  • Prioritise among different feature based innovations

Deep Blue+ exercise is not restricted to cigarette category alone. It can also be employed for similar understanding of price related behaviour for other smoking tobacco products like RYO, MYO, etc.

Governance

Deep Blue + falls under the governance of the Kantar Strategic Research Hub in India. All Deep Blue + studies are coordinated and analyzed by the specialist team. If you are conducting a Deep Blue + survey - your local Kantar coordinator should contact the Strategic Research Hub to discuss the specs and to obtain a quotation.

Deep Blue + is BAT Proprietary Methodology that uses Choice Based Modelling. It is not to be mixed up with the CBC product sold by other research agencies. Deep Blue + has been especially developed to suit the purposes of the tobacco category and the methodology is continuously under a validation programme.

In case of any questions on the Deep Blue + please contact your Kantar agency representative.
