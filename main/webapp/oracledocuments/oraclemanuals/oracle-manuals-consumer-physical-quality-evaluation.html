Overview

In -house standards of physical quality may differ from consumer expectations. The ORACLE Consumer Physical Quality Evaluation method (CPQE) is designed to analyse the consumer's perceived physical quality performance. The methodology is based on Qualitative focus groups followed by a Quantitative  Problem Detection Analysis to measure frequency of product defects and annoyance caused.

Consumer Physical Quality Evaluation (CPQE) is one of the two methods of Product Physical Evaluation alongside Psychophysical Analysis (PPA). Please see the attached documents for further Methodology information.

Objectives
  • Understand the consumer perception of product quality and its importance in brand selection, identifying the most important product quality attributes.
  • Categorise product quality defects regarding frequency of occurrence and annoyance caused, in order to focus quality efforts on those aspects that yield competitive advantages
