Overview

A Product Dialogue Workshop is an application of an Encounter Session. Encounter Sessions enable BAT personnel to enter into dialogue with consumers.  PDEW is a product -oriented session where discussion is focused upon taste and flavour. This allows BAT Brand Marketers and Product Developers to gain insights on product development directions in terms of taste/flavour and relevance to the brand.

Please see the attached documents for further Methodology information.

Objectives
  • To provide an opportunity for BAT Brand Marketers and Product Developers to enter into consumer dialogue with adult smokers in order to get insights on product development directions in terms of taste/ flavour and relevance to the brand.
  • To explore the brand and product inter-relationship.
